PRODUCT NAME: XOptimus Smart Charger (Wall Adapter)
MANUFACTURER: Hivericks Technologies Pvt Ltd (Chennai).
PRICE: ₹1499 INR.

CORE VALUE PROPOSITION:
Increases Li-Ion battery life expectancy and performance by up to 2x.
Prevents overcharging and heating issues using real-time parameter monitoring.
Extends the overall lifespan of your expensive devices.

KEY FEATURES:
- Smart Cut-off: Automatically disconnects power when the battery reaches the optimal level to prevent overheating.
- Heating Management: Built-in thermal sensors shut down the charger if internal or ambient temperatures exceed safe limits (Safe-Heat Technology).
- Battery Preservation: Minimizes micro-cycles during trickle charging.
- Smart Connect: Bluetooth 5.0 (BLE) for app-based monitoring.

MODES:
- Smart Mode (Default): Optimizes charging for long-term daily battery health.
- Gaming Mode: Specifically designed for high-end multimedia usage and gaming discharge rates. Highly suitable for gaming laptops and high-drain devices.
- Full Charge Mode: Standard charging to 100% when time is a priority.

TECHNICAL SPECS & COMPATIBILITY:
- Input: 110-240V AC (Standard).
- Output: Supports high-power delivery for laptops and smartphones.
- Plug Compatibility: Standard 3-pin and 2-pin compatibility. 
- 12A/15A Socket Compatibility: Yes, the XOptimus is designed to fit and work safely in standard 6A, 10A, 12A, and 16A (power) plug points commonly found in household and office environments.
- Laptop Suitability: Perfectly suitable for gaming laptop power strips and high-wattage adapters.

SAFETY:
- Protection against overcharging, voltage spikes, and short circuits.
- IP20 Protection Class.
- Fire-retardant industrial-grade casing.
